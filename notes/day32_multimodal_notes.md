# Day 32 Notes - Multi-Modal LLMs

**Date:** May 21, 2026

## What are Multi-Modal LLMs?

Models that can process multiple types of data (text + image + audio, etc.).

### Popular Models
- **BLIP**: Good for image captioning and VQA
- **LLaVA**: Large Language and Vision Assistant
- **CLIP**: Image-text matching
- **GPT-4V / Gemini**: Advanced commercial multi-modal models

## Key Capabilities
1. **Image Captioning**: Describe what is in an image
2. **Visual Question Answering (VQA)**: Answer questions about an image
3. **Image-Text Matching**: Find images that match text descriptions

## Major Takeaway
**"The future is multi-modal."**

Being able to understand both text and images makes AI much more useful for real-world applications (accessibility, content moderation, visual search, education, etc.).

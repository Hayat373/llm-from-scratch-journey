# Day 21 Log

**Date:** June 21, 2026

**Focus:** Deploying LLM Applications to Hugging Face Spaces

**What I Did**
- Learned how to deploy Gradio/RAG apps to Hugging Face Spaces
- Prepared the Ethiopian AI Assistant for public deployment
- Understood the deployment workflow (app.py, requirements.txt, Space creation)

**Key Learnings**
- Hugging Face Spaces is one of the easiest ways to deploy LLM apps publicly
- A good `app.py` + `requirements.txt` is enough for deployment
- Deployment is the final step that turns a notebook into a real product
- Public demos are extremely important for portfolios and hackathons

**Challenges**
- Model loading time and speed on Spaces (free tier has limitations)
- Managing dependencies and environment
- Keeping the app responsive

**Reflection**
Deployment is the bridge between "I built something" and "Others can use what I built". This is a crucial skill.

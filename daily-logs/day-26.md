# Day 26 Log

**Date:** June 25, 2026

**Focus:** Multi-Agent Systems

**What I Did**
- Learned the concept of Multi-Agent Systems
- Built a simple multi-agent setup (Researcher + Writer agent)
- Tested collaboration between agents
- Explored how agents can divide tasks and work together

**Key Learnings**
- Multi-Agent Systems involve multiple specialized LLMs/agents working together
- Common pattern: One agent gathers information (Researcher), another synthesizes it (Writer)
- Agents can simulate teamwork and divide complex tasks
- This approach often produces better results than a single LLM doing everything

**Challenges**
- Small models like TinyLlama are weak at consistent agent behavior
- Coordinating multiple agents is complex (prompt engineering, output parsing)
- Response time increases with more agents

**Reflection**
Multi-agent systems feel like the future of LLM applications. Even with small models, the idea of specialized agents collaborating is very powerful and impressive for portfolios.

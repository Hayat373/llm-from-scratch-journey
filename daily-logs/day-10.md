# Day 10 Log

**Date:** Jun 11, 2026

**Focus:** Advanced Prompt Engineering + Building a Useful AI Application

**What I Did**
- Learned advanced prompting techniques (System Prompt, Chain of Thought, Few-shot, Role Playing)
- Built a better domain-specific AI Assistant
- Experimented with different prompting strategies
- Compared quality of responses

**Key Learnings**
- System prompts are very powerful for controlling model behavior and personality
- Chain of Thought (CoT) prompting improves reasoning quality
- Few-shot examples help the model understand the desired output format
- Temperature, top_p, and good system prompts make a big difference in response quality
- Prompt engineering is one of the most important practical skills when working with LLMs

**Challenges**
- Finding the right balance for temperature and other parameters
- Some models still hallucinate or give repetitive answers
- Writing effective system prompts takes practice

**Reflection**
Prompt engineering turned a basic model into something much more useful. This is a skill I can immediately use in real projects and hackathons.
